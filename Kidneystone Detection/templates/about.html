About This System

The Kidney Stone Detection System is a web-based healthcare application designed to assist in early detection of kidney stones using artificial intelligence and image processing.

Why This System?

  • Kidney stones affect millions of people worldwide.
  • Early detection helps reduce complications.
  • AI helps analyze medical images faster.

Features

  • Upload kidney X-ray images
  • AI-based prediction
  • Prediction analysis charts
  • Health solutions and prevention tips
  • Doctor consultation suggestions

Project Purpose

This project demonstrates how artificial intelligence and machine learning can be integrated into healthcare systems to assist in medical diagnosis and improve patient care.
